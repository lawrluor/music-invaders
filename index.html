Music Invaders

How to Play

  • Use your MIDI instrument as a controller to shoot down baddies.
  • Each enemy corresponds to a specific note on your keyboard.
  • Play the note in the correct octave to shoot the enemy.
  • If playing in "chord mode": Play the chord in any octave to shoot the enemy.
  • Don't let enemies pass your shield - they'll hurt you!
  • Choose your notes wisely - running out of ammo ends the game.
